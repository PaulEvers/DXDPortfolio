Emotion Research

Emotion is a powerful tool that is used in virtually all stories. Therefore we were assigned the assignment to research the function of emotions in story. This research would give us a better understanding of how emotion is used in stories. For my research on emotions I focussed on emotions from a psychological perspective. My research can be found here. I found this research to be very insightful and made me more aware of how emotions are used when I was watching movies later on.

Pecha Kucha

A Pecha Kucha is a presentation that consists of 20 images. These images are meant to convey the general mood and tone of the story you want to tell. Each picture is shown for 20 second with an explanation of the presenter. To convey the tone and mood of our story, Jessica and I presented a Pecha Kucha.
