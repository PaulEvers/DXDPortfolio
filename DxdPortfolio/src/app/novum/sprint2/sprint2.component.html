More defining, and coming up with ideas.

Introduction

After our first sprint we got feedback that we needed more insight into our target group. So while in actuallity we had planned to start ideating in the second sprint, we decided to first do some more defining.

Once we had done this, we would move on to ideation.

Street Interviews

In the first sprint we wanted to do some street interviews of elderly people. Unfortunately due to a lack of time we were not able to do so. That is why we did it this sprint. Together with Jessica, we head to the center of Eindhoven to meet some people and have a quick conversation about digital media use and power of attorney. We found it a bit difficult to find people of our target group, which was made worse due to the Covid-19 pandemic. But eventually we found enough participants to come up with some meaningful insights.

Our preparation for the street interview looks as follows:

Short interview set up that can be used for interviews in streets, with people that are taking a bit of time to help us by answering some questions. These interviews should only take a minute or 10.

These questions are meant to be asked and afterwards use their answer to keep asking questions to get more information. Therefore it is possible that you might not get to all the questions because the people might run out of time.

Goals:

  1. Get to know whether people use DigiD
  2. Get to know whether people have experience with giving power of attorney
  3. What do they find difficult online and with computers/mobiles
  4. How do they think it will be improved

Methodology:

Use conversation starters in for example a park, supermarket, etc. to get information from the target audience in a normal conversation. (https://www.designkit.org/methods/conversation-starters)

  1. Introduce as students from Fontys
  2. Ask for a few minutes of their time
  3. We doen onderzoek naar het gebruik van Digid onder de mensen
  4. Gebruikt u Digid?
  5. Zou u uzelf als bekwaam met technologie noemen? / Maakt u veel gebruikt van technologie (mobiel, computer etc)?
  6. Loopt u weleens tegen problemen aan met het gebruik van technologie?
  7. Heeft u naasten zoals kleinkinderen of kinderen uw Digid zaken al eens laten doen?
  8. Weet u wat volmacht is?
  9. Heeft u al eens volmacht gegeven of gekregen?
  10. Wat zou u veranderen om het makkelijker te maken?
  11. Ask for age

The interviews we conducted can be found here. The insights we got from them go as follows:

  • The target group has more often received power of attorney rather than given it.
  • Most people have adequate knowledge about power of attorney.
  • Computers seem prefered over smartphones for DigiD.
  • They do not (want to) think about giving power of attorney yet.
  • Most do not like the idea of having their information online.
  • They would hire a notary for things they do not like to do, such as giving power of attorney.
