Welcome

Welcome to my DXD portfolio. For the last few months I have been following the Digital Experience Design minor at Fontys. Originally, I am a software engineering student, but during my study I became increasingly interested in design and human-technology interaction. That is why I chose to follow this minor.

On this website you can view each subject I followed. They include the exercises I did, what I learned from them, and a reflection. For my group project I worked for Novum. The Novum project is divided in four sprints.

I hope this portfolio gives you a greater insight into my learning progress over the last few months.
