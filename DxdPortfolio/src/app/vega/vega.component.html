Vega Collaboration

A collaboration with students from the South-African Vega School

Introduction

During the first weeks of my minor Digital Experience Design we got the oppurtunity to collaborate with the Johannesburg-based Vega School. Together with four other students, 2 who where from South-Africa, we worked on a project centering around transmedial world-building and storytelling. We were given the task to watch the documentary Before The Flood and build a transmedial world around it using three concepts. This page describes the process and our results of creating this world. Followed by a brief personal reflection on the assignment.

Research

During the first lessons we got paired with our teammates. Because of Covid-19 and the fact that we worked with students from South-Africa, it was logical to communicate and work together over Teams. The five of us got together and made some brief introductions. We set up our Google Drive and exchanged numbers to create a Whatsapp group. We quickly decided to each watch the documentary again and afterwards start analyzing together.

After we got back together we started analyzing the documentary and getting the most important information of the documentary in a single file. For us to create a successful transmedial campaign of Before the Flood, we needed get a proper understanding of the world and story the documentary was portraying. While watching the documentary we looked out for several things:

  • Locations: What locations where shown throughout the movie and why?
  • People: What people where presented, what did they talk about and add to the narrative?
  • Protagonist/Antagonist: Who or what was the hero of the story? And who or what was the villain.
  • Message: What is the overall message and goal of the documentary?

The results of our research document can be found here. This research would later help us find hooks in the original documentary to expand the transmedial universe. Along with this, the analysis helped us to start working on our campaign analysis.

For the campaign analysis we looked at the target audience we wanted to reach, created a problem statement, set up campaign goals, and started to think of concepts. The campaign analysis can be found here.

First concepts

After discussing the research we had a brain-storm session to come up with concepts for the transmedial campaign. From this session we chose our three favourite concepts and worked them further out.

Concept #1: Transmedial TV-Show
Title: The Flood
Main character: 16 year-old Island girl from Polynesia
Hook: Daughter of the woman that spoke at Paris Climate Conference
World: In the near future on a tropical island that heads from a eutopa to a dystopia
Migration: Prominent characters have fictional social media viewers can interact with + the characters of the show play the ‘After the Flood’ game + show starts with the moment mother of main character speaks at Paris Climate Conference + is watched by Zhang Wei.
Images
Concept #2: Youtube Influencer
Title: The Huang Zhang Channel
Main character: Chinese teenage up-and-coming gamer
Hook: Lives in the highly polluted city from the documentary
World: Set in current times without corona in a Chinese city plagued by smog
Migration: Character posts on Youtube channel where it interacts with his viewers + watches the TV-show + is sponsored by game developers of ‘After the Flood’
Images
Concept #3: AR-Game
Title: After the Flood
Main character: The player
Hook: Panels from start of documentary
World: Set in 100 years from after the docu. Set in the real world mixed with a digital one. Starts as a dystopia, heads into a utopia depending on the player.
Migration: Title refers to documentary + is played by the tv-show characters as well as Zhang Wei.
Images

We presented these concepts to our classmates and teachers in a presentation.
